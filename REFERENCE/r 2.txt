1. Caprese Salad Skewers:
Ingredients:

Cherry tomatoes
Fresh mozzarella balls
Fresh basil leaves
Balsamic glaze
Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Thread a cherry tomato, a mozzarella ball, and a basil leaf onto a toothpick or skewer.
Arrange the skewers on a serving plate.
Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
Serve and enjoy these bite-sized Caprese Salad Skewers!
2. Pasta Aglio e Olio:
Ingredients:

Spaghetti or your favorite pasta
Olive oil
Garlic cloves, thinly sliced
Red pepper flakes (optional)
Parsley, chopped
Grated Parmesan cheese
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Reserve some pasta water before draining.
In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add sliced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using). Saute until the garlic is golden but not burnt.
Add the cooked pasta to the pan, tossing to coat in the garlic-infused oil. If it's too dry, add a bit of reserved pasta water.
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Toss in chopped parsley.
Serve with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese on top.

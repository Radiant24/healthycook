Avocado Toast
Ingredients

1 ripe avocado
2 slices of bread
Salt and pepper to taste
Optional toppings cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, poached egg
Instructions

Toast the slices of bread to your liking.
While the bread is toasting, mash the ripe avocado in a bowl with a fork.
Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread slices.
Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.
Add any optional toppings you like, such as sliced cherry tomatoes, a pinch of red pepper flakes, or a poached egg.
One-Pan Lemon Garlic Chicken
Ingredients

2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 lemon (juiced)
3 cloves garlic (minced)
2 tablespoons olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
Fresh herbs (such as rosemary or thyme), chopped (optional)
Instructions

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish.
In a small bowl, mix together the lemon juice, minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
Pour the lemon garlic mixture over the chicken breasts, ensuring they are well coated.
Optionally, sprinkle fresh herbs on top.
Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
Fruit Salad
Ingredients

Assorted fruits (e.g., strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, pineapple, grapes)
1 tablespoon honey (optional)
Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)
Instructions

Wash and chop the fruits into bite-sized pieces.
In a bowl, combine the assorted fruits.
Drizzle honey over the fruit salad if you desire a bit of sweetness.
Toss the fruits gently to combine.
Garnish with fresh mint leaves if you like.
